LebN

An open-source, privacy-focussed healthcare helper

What We Do

LebN (Lee-ben) is a privacy focussed healthcare assistant, which takes a user-created health profile and analyzes the data with respect to a multitude of public health APIs, such as the World Health Organization, in a private way.

Privacy Matters to Us

The data provided by the users in building their individual health profile will be stored strictly on the users device and will not be stored in our servers, meaning that there is no way for your private data to be sold or stolen.

Secure. Easy. Open-Source. 

We pride ourselves with providing convenient and accurate health news reporting, personalized to you to support your busy lifestyle
